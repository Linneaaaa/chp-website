About CHP

Where Precision
Meets Elegance

Founded in 2012, CHP (Shanghai Chuanhe Packaging Container Co., Ltd.) is a global manufacturer specializing in premium plastic bottles for cosmetics, personal care, and household goods.

Located at No.88 Zhaojiajing Road, Songjiang District, Shanghai, our facility employs advanced injection-stretch-blow molding processes to deliver bottles that are as beautiful as what they contain.

ISO 9001 ISO 14001 ISO 45001 BRCGS
Full Customization
Shape, color, material, decoration — fully tailored.
Strict Quality Control
Multi-stage QC process every batch.
Fast Lead Times
Lean production and in-house tooling.
Global Reach
Exporting to Europe, North America, Asia.
